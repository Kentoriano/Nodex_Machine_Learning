Step 5 — Cluster Interpretation
Cluster 0 — Inactive / Low Activity

Low balance, very low purchases. Dormant or rarely used credit cards. Likely new customers or accounts that were opened but never actively used.

Cluster 1 — Cash Advance Users

High cash advance transactions, low purchases. These customers use the card as a source of cash rather than for shopping. Higher financial risk.

Cluster 2 — Active Buyers

High purchase frequency, high payment rate, low cash advances. These customers shop frequently and pay their balance. Most profitable segment.

Cluster 3 — High Balance Accumulators

High balance and credit limit with moderate payments. These customers carry large outstanding balances month to month. Generate interest revenue but represent credit risk.
