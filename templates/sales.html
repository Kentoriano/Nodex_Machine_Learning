Smart Sales Prediction

Problem Description

Many businesses struggle to accurately predict future product demand. Decisions are often based on intuition rather than data analysis.

This leads to problems such as excess inventory, product shortages, and financial losses that directly affect profitability.

Without predictive tools, companies cannot anticipate changes in customer behavior.

Proposed Solution

A machine learning model, such as linear regression, can analyze historical sales data to identify patterns and predict future demand.

  • Improved inventory planning
  • Reduction of losses
  • Better decision-making
  • Optimized resource management

This allows businesses to make decisions based on data instead of assumptions.
