Video game use case

Machine Learning in video games is used to create adaptive AI, meaning enemies or characters that learn from the player’s behavior and adjust their strategy in real time.

Instead of following fixed programmed patterns, the AI analyzes how you play (aggressive, defensive, fast explorer, etc.) and modifies its difficulty.

Streaming recommendation use case

Machine Learning in Netflix is used to recommend movies and series based on user behavior. The platform analyzes what you watch, how long you watch it, and what you skip.

Instead of showing the same content to everyone, the system predicts what each user may like, creating a personalized experience that increases engagement and retention.
